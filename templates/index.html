What is Kashima Coat?

Kashima Coat is Fox's proprietary gold-colored Diamond-like Carbon (DLC) coating applied to stanchion tubes. It provides ultra-low friction, extended durability, and the smoothest suspension feel available.

  • Ultra-low friction surface
  • Superior oil retention
  • Extended service life
  • Signature gold appearance
