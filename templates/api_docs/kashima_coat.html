What is Kashima Coat?

Kashima is Fox's premium surface coating. You'll see it on their top-tier suspension.

What It Does

Kashima coating is a hard-anodized finish that reduces friction inside the stanchions (the inner tubes). Less friction means smoother suspension action and better small-bump sensitivity.

What It Looks Like

Kashima-coated parts have a distinctive shiny, almost gold-tinted surface. You can't miss it.

Non-Kashima parts have a matte black finish.

Does It Matter?

For most riders, the difference is small. But if you're chasing every bit of performance, Kashima is worth it. It also resists scratches better and keeps looking new longer.
