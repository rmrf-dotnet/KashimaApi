Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the API.

What data do you have?

Fox suspension forks, shocks, and dropper posts from 2015 to present.

Is the data accurate?

We pull directly from Fox's official specifications. We verify measurements against physical products when possible.

Can I cache the data?

Yes! Caching is encouraged. Just re-fetch once a week to get updates.

Do you have older parts?

We focus on current models. Some older data is available - try searching without year filters.

How do I report bad data?

Contact us through the support page. We appreciate the help keeping data accurate.
