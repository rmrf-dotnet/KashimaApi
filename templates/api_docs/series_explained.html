Factory vs Performance Series

Fox makes two main lines of suspension. Here's how they differ.

Factory Series

The top-tier line. Here's what you get:

  • • Kashima-coated stanchions
  • • Premium dampers
  • • More adjustability
  • • Lighter weight

Performance Series

The value line. Here's what's different:

  • • Standard black coating
  • • Solid dampers
  • • Less adjustability
  • • Heavier
  • • Lower price

Which Should You Get?

For most riders, Performance is fine. If you want the best performance, or if budget isn't a problem, Factory is worth it. The difference is real but not huge.
