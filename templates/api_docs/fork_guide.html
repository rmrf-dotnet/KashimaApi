Fork Selection Guide

32 SC

Cross-country racing. Lightest. 32mm stanchions. 100mm only.

34 SC

Cross-country to trail. Step-Cast saves weight. 100-120mm travel.

36

All-mountain. 36mm stanchions. 140-160mm travel. Most versatile.

38

Enduro racing. 38mm stanchions. 160-180mm travel.

40

Downhill racing. Dual crown. 190-203mm travel.
