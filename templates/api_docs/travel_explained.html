Understanding Travel

Travel is how much your suspension can compress. Choosing the right amount matters.

100-120mm

Cross country and light trail riding. Fast, efficient, but limited capability on rough trails.

130-150mm

Trail and all-mountain. The sweet spot for most riders. Good for climbing, handles descents well.

160-180mm

Enduro and aggressive trail. Absorbs big hits, but bobs more when climbing. Heavy.

200mm+

Downhill and bike park. Maximum plushness. Not for pedaling much. For lift-accessed runs.

How to Choose

More travel is more fun on descents but costs efficiency climbing. Match your riding style. If you climb more than you descend, go lower. If you live for the downhill, go higher.
