Understanding Specs

Stanchion Diameter

The inner tube diameter: 32, 34, 36, 38, 40mm. Thicker = stiffer = more downhill capability.

Travel

How much the suspension compresses in mm. More travel = more big hit capability.

Offset/Rake

How far the wheel is in front of the steerer. More offset = more stable, less trail.

Eye-to-Eye

Shock measurement from mount to mount. Must match your frame eyelet spacing.
